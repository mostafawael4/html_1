coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain flowering plants in the coffee genus. from the coffee fruit , the seeds are separated to produce a stable , row product: unroasted green coffee. the seeds are then roasted , aprocess which transforms them into a consumable product: roasted coffee, which is ground into fine particles that are tipically steeped in hot water before being filtered out , producing a cup of coffee .
